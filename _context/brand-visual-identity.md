# Brand visual identity (Gallivant)

Fictional example aligned with [reference/gallivant-origin.md](reference/gallivant-origin.md). Observable marketing facts below; human decisions marked explicitly.

## logo

Wordmark **Gallivant** in a friendly serif-styled logotype (marketing) and plain sans in product UI. No aggressive travel clichés (no globes with airplanes in primary mark).

source: human (fictional example)

## colors

| Role | Hex | Usage |
|------|-----|--------|
| Warm navy | `#1E3A4C` | Headers, primary text |
| Road-trip terracotta | `#C4694F` | Accent, CTAs, illustration highlights |
| Sand | `#F5F0E8` | Page background — warm, not clinical |
| Leaf green | `#4A7C59` | Secondary accent, success states |
| Muted | `#6B6560` | Captions |

Marketing skews warm and human (~60% sand background, 25% navy type, 15% terracotta/green accents).

source: human (fictional example)

## typography

- **Marketing headings:** Fraunces or similar soft serif (welcoming, not bank-core)
- **Body:** Source Sans 3, 17px base — readable, conversational
- **Product UI:** Inter for density in Gallivant View

source: human (fictional example)

## style

- Photography: real travelers in motion (airport, hotel lobby, customer site) — candid, not stock-handshake.
- Illustration: simple line drawings — **including the small sandwich** on the welcome card and occasional empty-state art.
- Homepage social proof: large pull quotes in Alice/Becca-approved customer words; minimal logo bars.
- Finance content (View): cleaner, calmer layouts — still warm, fewer illustrations.

source: gallivant-origin (sandwich card) + human (fictional example)

## welcome card (canonical artifact)

- Format: folded card, envelope, stamp — not a digital postcard
- Corner illustration: small sandwich (Alice-designed)
- Inside copy: *Welcome to Gallivant. We're glad you're here. Let's go.*
- Sent by Travel Companions at every customer go-live

source: gallivant-origin (verbatim)

## avoid

- Cold fintech blue gradients and "enterprise glass" UI in marketing
- Imagery that frames employees as careless spenders
- Replacing the welcome card with an automated email-only onboarding

source: human (fictional example)

## admired-references

- **Patagonia** — warmth and directness in copy tone (aspiration only)
- **Monzo early brand** — human support as visible product feature (aspiration only)

source: human (fictional example)
