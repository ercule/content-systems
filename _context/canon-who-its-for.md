## icp

### Profile: enterprise with frequent travelers

**Account**

- 500+ employees; material travel and T&E spend
- Existing T&E or card program that finance distrusts and employees resent
- Trigger: reimbursement complaints, audit pain, or failed rollout of a legacy suite

**Champion: VP Finance or Head of Travel & Expense**

- Job to be done: policy compliance and clean books without becoming the villain to every road warrior
- Alternatives: legacy T&E suite (slow UX, heavy services); consumer booking tools plus spreadsheets (no control)
- Objections: "We just reimplemented Concur" — Gallivant wins on speed, capture-in-the-moment, and Travel Companions onboarding

**Champion: Alice's first-30-days touch** — new accounts get founder-level support until workflows stick; objections surface early in the 84-page feedback doc, not at renewal.

source: derived from gallivant-origin + human (fictional example)
