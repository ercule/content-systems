## unique-attributes

Overview: Gallivant optimizes for speed and humanity in a category known for neither.

1. **Policy-native booking** — normal trips book inside company rules; travelers skip permission theater for routine travel.
2. **Capture in the moment** — expenses record while the traveler is still there, not from a Sunday-night receipt pile.
3. **Fast reimbursement** — out-of-pocket spend returns quickly because employees fronted company money.
4. **Finance clarity without suspicion** — full visibility for finance; travelers don't feel like suspects over everyday spend.
5. **Travel Companions** — dedicated human support team (not "Customer Success"); Alice personally handles every new account's tickets for the first 30 days.

source: derived from gallivant-origin (Alice product narrative + Travel Companions)

## value-proposition

**One line (Alice):** Book inside policy, capture while you're there, get paid back fast, and let finance see everything — without making the person who expensed a sandwich feel like a suspect.

**One line (Becca):** It's really fast and it works.

**Strands:**

- **Traveler experience:** Policy-compliant booking and instant capture replace email chains and receipt archaeology.
- **Finance control:** Gallivant View delivers audit-ready visibility without punitive UX.
- **Human support:** Named people respond within the hour when something breaks — not a ticket black hole.

source: gallivant-origin (verbatim Alice/Becca quotes + synthesis)

## how-it-works

1. Employee books travel inside company policy in Gallivant — routine trips need no extra approval.
2. At point of spend, Gallivant captures the expense (receipt, card feed, or manual snap) before the traveler moves on.
3. Finance sets rules and thresholds in Gallivant View; exceptions route to approvers with context attached.
4. Reimbursement runs on a predictable cadence; employees aren't floating company spend for weeks.
5. Travel Companions onboard new accounts — handwritten welcome card at go-live, Alice on tickets for days 1–30.

source: derived from gallivant-origin
