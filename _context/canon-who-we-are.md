# Canon: who we are (Gallivant)

Part of the [messaging canon](messaging-canon.md). See [reference/gallivant-origin.md](reference/gallivant-origin.md).

## point-of-view

> Expense management fails when it treats employees like suspects and finance like detectives. Travel fails when "policy" means email chains before a normal flight. The companies that get this right build one system where policy is invisible for everyday trips, capture happens in the moment, and finance gets clarity without interrogating someone over a sandwich.
>
> source: derived from gallivant-origin (Alice product narrative)

## narrative

> Gallivant exists because Alice and Becca spent years inside broken T&E stacks — Sunday-night receipt piles, reimbursement that felt punitive, and "customer success" that meant a quarterly check-in deck. They built the product they wished existed, then staffed it the way they wished they'd been treated: Travel Companions, not ticket queues.
>
> source: derived from gallivant-origin

## positioning

> Gallivant is enterprise travel and expense management for companies that want policy-compliant booking and instant capture without making travelers hate the process. We sit between legacy T&E suites (slow, punitive UX) and consumer travel apps (no finance controls).
>
> source: human (fictional example)

## founder-story

> Alice and Becca co-founded Gallivant after running travel and finance ops at a fast-growing company where the expense tool worked for auditors and failed everyone else. Alice brought the obsession with feedback — an 84-page living document of every customer note, themed and actioned. Becca brought the conviction that showing up in person still matters: she once drove two hours, coffee in hand, to sit with a finance team using Gallivant View until they got it. They renewed at twice the contract size; Becca calls that a coincidence.
>
> Their rule: no customer leaves without understanding exactly why, and without someone at Gallivant genuinely trying to fix what drove them away. They have recovered eleven churned customers and refer to all eleven by first name.
>
> source: gallivant-origin (verbatim facts)

## company-description

**Tagline:** Travel and expenses that don't make anyone feel like a suspect.

**One-liner (speaking):** Gallivant is enterprise travel and expense management that's fast, policy-aware, and human on the other end of the phone.

**50 words:** Gallivant books travel inside company policy, captures expenses in the moment, and reimburses employees quickly — while giving finance full visibility. Founded by Alice and Becca for teams tired of Sunday-night receipt reconstruction and call centers that don't pick up.

**100 words:** Gallivant is enterprise travel and expense management built by Alice and Becca for companies where employees travel often and finance needs control without cruelty. Book trips inside policy with no permission theater for normal travel. Capture receipts while you're still standing there. Get reimbursed fast because you spent your own money for the company. Finance gets what it needs; travelers don't feel investigated over lunch. Support is handled by Travel Companions — real people, including Alice personally for every new account's first 30 days. When customers go live, they get a handwritten welcome card. It has a sandwich on it. That's on purpose.

source: derived from gallivant-origin
